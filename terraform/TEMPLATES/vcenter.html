CKO will optionally connect to a VCenter and deploy a K8s cluster

The k8s cluster will automatically be configured with Calico using non overlay mode and leveraging BGP to advertise routes to ACI leaves
If you prefer CKO not to configure the K8s cluster, CKO can provide you the Calico BGP configuration file to provide to your K8s installer
