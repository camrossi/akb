Here you should insert the L3out details

CKT will configure a Floating L3out in the ACI fabric.

The Floating L3out will be used to peer the K8s nodes through Calico BGP
Calico BGP nodes will advertise their subnet supernet, services and pod subnet to the ACI fabric
A few parameters to define the floating L3out should be defined. Advanced L3out is optionally configurable
Note: The Tenant, VRF and Physical Domain needs to be pre-configured on ACI.
